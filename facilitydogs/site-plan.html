Content

Home page

Working police dogs When it comes to Law Enforcement dogs one thinks of German shepherds and high drive dogs like Belgian Malinois that find fleeing criminals, sniff out drugs or explosives. One doesn’t really think of a calm well mannered dog that is there to help with the stress victims feel during a criminal investigation and the officers that investigate those crimes. The dogs are called facility dogs that under go a strict breeding and training program in order to be utilized in these environments

Service dogs Official service dogs have been around from the early 1900’s and have developed in so many ways. One of those ways is a facility dog. A facility dog is breed and trained through a long strict process in order to be teamed up with a handler and certified

Images for the Home page

Dog Breeders

Canin Companions for Independence is a national organization that produces these furry little friends and it starts with the breeders. A cross the country there are breeders of Labradors, golden retrievers and golden Labrador mix dogs that have been vetted out for this very purpose. Volunteers put in the work to breed and care for the dogs until the puppies can be weaned from the mother. After they are weaned, each puppy goes to a puppy raiser.

Images for the Page 2

Puppy Raiser

The puppy raiser is a volunteer who welcomes the puppy into their home for next 18 months of their life. This is where the dog starts to learns the basics. All the dogs are crate trained and work on leash. They are also evaluated for any signs of aggression or behaviors that could be concerning.

Official Training

At the end of the 18 months the dogs next step is to bet taken to a facility where they work with a professional dog trainer for the next six months. By the end of the six months the dogs are trained in up to 30 to 40 commands that can assist persons with disabilities and well-mannered enough to work in facilities settings like schools, physical therapy, counselors’ office, court houses and Law Enforcements victim advocacy Centers
